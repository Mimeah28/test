schnitzel

this is a classic Israeli food made all over th country. filt chicken breast coated in breadcrumbs and deep friedin oil.

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1 box of breadcrumbs
  • 1 bottle of oil
  • 4 eggs
  • any spices you like

Instructions

  1. flit the chicken breast
  2. crack the eggs into a bowl and mix them
  3. pour the breadcrumbs onto a plate
  4. set a slice of chicken breast into the egg and them set it on to he breadcrumbs cover it completely making sure when you lift it up it is completelyoated in breadcrumbs
